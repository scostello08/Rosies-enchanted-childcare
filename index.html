Infant Care

Rosie provides the highest quality care in an environment that is designed with the parent and child in mind. While in Rosie's care, the children are stimulated through music, exercise, outdoor blanket time, facial and verbal mimicking as well as lots of hugs, tickles, and sunggles. In addition, Rosie helps your child to tackle motor skills, realize the power of language, and begin to understand this new world from many angles.

Toddler Care

At this age, children are meeting new challenges as fast as they come. Crawling, walking and eventually running are levels they accomplish in what seems like a blink of an eye. Children learn best if they are in a stimulating environment. Music, stories, outdoor play, and learning simplicities envelop much of the day. Rosie is dedicated in helping each child develop these skills through a trusting and loving relationship.

Preschool Care

Preschool is an essential stepping stone to kindergarten success. They will begin to play with others, form friendships, pretend play, and become more independent. Rosie ensures that children achieve significant development advancements, learn effective communication, and develop collaborative skills, independently and in group settings. Activities include art, discovery centers, dress up, outdoor play and much more.
